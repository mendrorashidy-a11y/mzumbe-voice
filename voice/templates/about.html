📜 Our History

Mzumbe University started as the Institute of Development Management (IDM) in 1953. In 2001, it was granted full university status by the Government of Tanzania. Today, it operates three campuses: the main campus in Morogoro, the Mbeya Campus College, and the Dar es Salaam Campus College.

The university has produced thousands of graduates who serve as leaders in government, private sector, and civil society across Tanzania and beyond.
